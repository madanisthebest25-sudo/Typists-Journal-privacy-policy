Privacy Policy for "The Typist's Journal"

Effective Date: 22/08/2025

This privacy policy describes how "The Typist's Journal" ("the Skill") works and how it handles your information. The Skill is designed to be a personal analytics tool and does not collect, store, or share any of your personal data.

1. Information We Collect

The Skill does not collect any personal information. It uses information provided by Amazon solely to fulfill your voice requests. We do not store your name, voice recordings, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

The Skill does not collect, save, or transmit any data that could be used to identify you, including:

  • Your name, email address, or phone number.
  • Your Amazon account information.
  • Your location data.
  • Any voice data or recordings, which are handled exclusively by Amazon.

3. Data Storage

All keystroke data analyzed by the Skill is stored exclusively on your local machine and is not transmitted to our servers or any third-party services. The Skill only processes this local data when you make a specific voice request.

4. How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect any personal information, we do not use your information for any purpose, including marketing, advertising, or profiling.

5. Third-Party Services

The Skill operates on the Amazon Alexa platform. By using the Skill, you are also subject to the privacy policies and terms of service of Amazon.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy here.
